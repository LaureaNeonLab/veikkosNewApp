Noodle recipe app

App contains different noodle recipes. User can share the recipes they like in social media and they can submit their own recipes which need to approved before they are included into the app.

Login (with FB, TW, G)
Search for the recipe
Comment, like, share the recipe
Submit own recipes
Logout
